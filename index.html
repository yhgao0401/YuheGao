About Me

Greetings! Here is Yuhe, a data scientist with a background in geographic information science and information science. My research focuses on leveraging data science, machine learning, and statistical methods to address public health challenges, aiming to bridge complex theories with real-world healthcare solutions.

    Research Interest:
  • Data-driven healthcare approaches to clinical datasets, especially for modeling;
  • Transfer learning;
  • Multimodal data integration (EHRs, medical imaging, geospatial data).
